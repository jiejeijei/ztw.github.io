What is true friendship? It is not about how many friends you have, but about the quality of the connection. A true friend is someone who listens to you when you are happy or sad. They offer support when things get tough and share joy in your successes.

Friendship is like a small boat on the ocean. When the sea is calm, you enjoy the ride together. When storms come, you help each other to stay safe and strong. Remember, being a good friend is just as important as having one. It requires trust, honesty, and kindness. Treasure your friends, for they make your life journey more beautiful.
